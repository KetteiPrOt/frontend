white-space

Define como se trataran los saltos de linea y espacios en el codigo, al renderizar el texto.

[normal] El texto se envuelve y los espacios y saltos de linea se colapsan.

Hey everyone! It's almost 2022 and we still don't know if there is aliens living among us, or do we? Maybe the person writing this is an alien. You will never know.

[nowrap] El texto no se envuelve y los espacios y saltos de linea se colapsan.

Hey everyone! It's almost 2022 and we still don't know if there is aliens living among us, or do we? Maybe the person writing this is an alien. You will never know.

[pre] Preserva los espacios y saltos de linea del elemento. El texto no se envuelve.

Hey everyone! It's almost 2022 and we still don't know if there is aliens living among us, or do we? Maybe the person writing this is an alien. You will never know.

[pre-line] Preserva los saltos de linea, pero no los espacios del elemento. El texto se envuelve.

Hey everyone! It's almost 2022 and we still don't know if there is aliens living among us, or do we? Maybe the person writing this is an alien. You will never know.

[pre-wrap] Preserva los saltos de linea, pero no los espacios del elemento. El texto se envuelve.

Hey everyone! It's almost 2022 and we still don't know if there is aliens living among us, or do we? Maybe the person writing this is an alien. You will never know.
